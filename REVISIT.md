## Format & house rules — strengthen the disclosure-flexibility framing

**Where:** `/event` page, "Format & house rules" section in
[app/event/page.tsx](app/event/page.tsx).

**Today it reads:**

> Sessions may run under Chatham House rule where the company prefers it;
> the write-up is reviewed before publication. Recording where the company
> permits.

**Proposed stronger copy:**

> Practitioner to practitioner. Where a company can't share specifics, we
> abstract them — the engineering insight is what travels, not the logos.
> Sessions can run under Chatham House rule; write-ups are reviewed before
> publication; recording only where the company permits.

**Why this matters:** Some companies can't fully disclose a live production
system (regulated environments, NDAs, security boundaries). The current
copy quietly notes Chatham House rule as a caveat; the proposed copy turns
it into a confident inclusion — "the engineering insight is what travels,
not the logos" — so the strong production-stories pitch in the hero
doesn't accidentally exclude speakers who can't name the company.

**Status:** Held. Revisit when reviewing pages end-to-end.
